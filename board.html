The President is the head of the board and typically holds the highest position in an organization. They are responsible for leading the board, setting strategic direction, representing the organization externally, and overseeing its overall operations.
Tracey Rosario, Vice President

The Vice President assists in all aspects and may step in to fulfill President responsibilities when needed. They work together with the President and execute the organization's goals and initiatives.
Courtney Sharp, Director

Directors are board members who provide guidance, expertise, and oversight for specific areas or functions within the organization. They may be responsible for specific committees or departments, bringing their knowledge and experience to help shape the organization's strategies and policies.
Natalie Olivia Titus, Ambassador

An Ambassador, in this context, refers to an individual who represents the organization externally, often in diplomatic or public relations roles. They may establish and maintain relationships with stakeholders, promote the organization's interests, and advocate for its mission and values.
