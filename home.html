About Us

The Unspoken Belle is a compassionate non-profit organization dedicated to creating brighter futures for underprivileged children and teenagers. With a focus on Haiti, we provide essential tuition ...assistance and school supplies to ensure that every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to thrive. Understanding the unique challenges faced by teenage girls in foster care, our foundation offers not only hygiene products but also valuable mentorship programs. These initiatives are designed to empower young women with the confidence and skills they need to navigate life's challenges with grace and resilience. Additionally, we take pride in offering pageant and etiquette training for teenage girls, fostering self-esteem and social skills that will benefit them in all areas of life. For the young men in foster homes, we provide free back-to-school haircuts, helping them to start their academic year with a sense of pride and self-assurance. At The Unspoken Belle, we believe in nurturing the whole child, providing the tools and support needed for all young people to rise above their circumstances and achieve their dreams.
Mission Statement

The Unspoken Belle is dedicated to illuminating paths of opportunity for underprivileged children and teenagers in Haiti, by breaking down the barriers to education ...and personal development. Our mission is to ensure that every child, regardless of their circumstances, has access to the education, resources, and mentorship they need to flourish. Through tuition assistance, supply of school essentials, and targeted support programs, we aim to empower the youth with the foundation for a brighter, more promising future.
Vision Statement

Our vision is a world where every child and teenager, especially those in the shadows of poverty and neglect, can access the education and personal growth ...opportunities they deserve. We envision a future where the young individuals we support in Haiti and beyond can overcome their challenges, realize their potential, and contribute positively to their communities. By nurturing their education, self-esteem, and life skills, we strive to create a ripple effect of empowerment, hope, and change that transcends generations.
